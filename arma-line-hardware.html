About the Company

Powering the Power Sector

ARMA Line Hardware & Accessories Ltd. was established in 2006 to serve the increasing demand for quality line hardware and accessories in Bangladesh's rapidly expanding power sector.

Founded as a sister concern to ARMA Electric Company, we were specifically built to strengthen our group's procurement capacity — and we deliver on that every year. We operate from two strategic locations, enabling efficient supply across the country.
