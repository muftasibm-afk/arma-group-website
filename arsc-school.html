✨ Our Story

A school built on heart and purpose

Abdur Razzaque School & College began in 2008 in Banasree, Dhaka — founded with one clear aim: to create a school that is genuinely different.

We started with 180 children and 20 teachers. Today, through perseverance and the support of parents who believed in us, we have grown to over 550 students and 40 teachers — and we're just getting started.

ARSC welcomes boys and girls regardless of religious or political beliefs, national origin, or financial status. Every child deserves a chance to shine.
