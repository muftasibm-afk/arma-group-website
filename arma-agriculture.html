About Us

Rooted in Ashulia,
growing nationwide

ARMA Agriculture Limited began its journey in 2005 with a clear plan: produce agro-based value-added products for both local and international markets. We chose Ashulia — one of Bangladesh's richest agricultural regions — as our home.

Our main site covers 1,400 decimals at Sholeasha Bazar, adjacent to the Ashulia-Saidpur highway and beside the historic Tista Barage Canal. We have government permission for 40 acres total, with 21 already secured.

We are also establishing a dairy farm in Munshiganj — bringing fresh, quality dairy products closer to Dhaka's families.
