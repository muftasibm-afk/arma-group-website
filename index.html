Who We Are

A CONGLOMERATE
BUILT ON PRINCIPLE

ARMA Group began in 1985 as a single electrical construction firm. Over four decades, through deliberate and patient growth, we have become one of Bangladesh's most trusted multi-sector conglomerates — without ever compromising the founding values of fairness, honesty, and genuine care.

Today, seven sister concerns operate under the ARMA umbrella. Each serves the development of Bangladesh. Together, they form a group that has earned the trust of the country's most important institutions.

Every decision rooted in fairness and honesty since day one.
