COMPANY OVERVIEW

TRUSTED BY
BANGLADESH'S
POWER SECTOR

ARMA Electric Company is the flagship concern of ARMA Group — started in 1985 as a civil and electrical construction enterprise in Bangladesh's power sector. We began with civil works for Government bodies and quickly grew to execute transmission lines, distribution lines, and substation construction.

A landmark achievement was the construction of a 230KV transmission line including its Control Room Building under the Power Grid Company of Bangladesh (PGCB). Today we hold ISO 9001:2015 certification and serve the country's most critical infrastructure.
